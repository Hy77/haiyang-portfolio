About Me

I'm a Mechatronics Engineer with practical experience across robotics systems, computer vision, and hardware prototyping. I like building engineering solutions that move beyond concept stage and work reliably in real environments.

My background spans mechanical design, sensor integration, control logic, and perception pipelines. Most of my projects sit at the boundary between hardware and software, where system integration matters as much as the individual components themselves.

I graduated from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) in Mechatronics Engineering and am especially interested in robotics, automation, and deployable vision-based systems.
